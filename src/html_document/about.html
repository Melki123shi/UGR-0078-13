About Us

We are a non-governmental organization which works focusing on caring and supporting particularly abandoned and sick elderly people. This organization was started by a selfless person called Ato Sintayehu with nothing but a dream to help the people who are unable to care for them selves. We take good care of the people who we have picked up from the streets with no one to help and pick them up. This organization gives a second chance of life for those who gave up on there lives.
